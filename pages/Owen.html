About Me

Learning Guitar

Since a young age I've been infactuated with music it was my entire life. I've spent my whole life exploring music and travelling to different concerts and festivals. Growing up my dad played some of the greats like Jimi Hendrix, this is what inspired me to learn the guitar.

Around age 12 I started learning the guitar and ukulele starting with some of my favourite songs from Arctic Monkeys, Radiohead and Weezer, and I practiced everyday until I got really good

Growing up with tech

Around the same age I started playing the guitar I started learning about PC's and tech. My dad is an engineer and he is where I got most of my interests from. He allowed me to tinker and offered loads of old parts to play around with teaching me as I went along.

At age 15 I built my first PC with the help of my dad and youtube and I slowly upgraded it using money I earned from my paper round and woring weekends at a chinese.

Now I have a complete setup including a wall mounted top of the rage PC, high end XLR mic and interface, 5.1 surround sound and a beautiful electric guitar and amp to explore my passions.
