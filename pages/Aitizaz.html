About me

Well you clicked on this link to know everything about me, well not everything just some things. You are still reading, this oh well I guess I have to tell you a little something about me.

Let’s start off with my name which is Aitizaz, and I welcome you to this page about me.


Believe it or not this kid on the left is me, I know haven’t changed much apart from my age!!! When this picture was taken it was roughly when my dad got my first console the Nintendo DSi for my birthday and a remote-control car. You might be thinking as to why I am mentioning this but hang on I will explain why right now. Fast forward a couple of months later my R.C car broke and decided to fix it myself, so I grabbed my dad’s toolbox and started dismantling the car and found a motor, battery and a chip board from there I dismantled the controller and was fascinated by how such a small board can control a car. This sparked my interest in technology and from there I would always dismantle anything technology related and would research what each component did.
Fun fact: I tried to take my dad’s laptop apart when I was 9 and let’s just say that dad never got to see that computer ever again.

My first experience with 'programming' wasn’t really a program language but a software called ‘Scratch’ where you drag and drop things with minor editing to create a game in which I did and was interested in making a game where you hit a bunch of circles which outputs the sounds of drums as I was in a band in primary school playing the drums.

Playing the drums was something I liked but stopped playing when I reached secondary school where I learnt a bit more about computers and decided that I would study Computer Science as one of subjects at A-Levels and possibly help land me a job to with programming (Spoiler Alert: it did!!). During A-levels, I have been working on many projects such as creating my first ever game in the program language C# which I must say is one of the achievements that I am proud of as it was interesting how you can create something to life with just coding.
